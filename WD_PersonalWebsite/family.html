My Family

I presently have a family of 5. There's me, My 2 younger sisters, and my parents. My mom works the day

shift as a registered nurse, while my dad works the night shift at his job, so usually their schedules

contradict each other and I only see one of them at a time. As a result of this, there's a good percentage

of the time where I act as the babysitter for my two younger sisters whenever something's come up and

my parents need to step away from my sisters.

 

 

 

 

 

 

My Sisters

Presently, My sisters Evelyn and Cecelia are in kindergarten and 1st grade. They both attend Park Lane

Elementary, and for the most part they get along fine (key words:"for the most part"). Evelyn is the middle

sister, with her being 7 years old, while Cecelia is the youngest, being 6 years old. Personality wise,

Evelyn is more of the "popular kid" type, with her actually being one of the more popular kids at her

school, while Cece is more like me in which she tends to keep more to herself, but she doesn't hesitate to

put herself out there depending on the circumstance. An interesting fun fact, but Cece is actually ahead of

her class in multiple skills by at least 1-2 grade levels (pretty impressive!).
